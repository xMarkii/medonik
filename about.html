What about us?

Our purpose

As travellers, nothing infuriates us more than getting sick and ruining a wonderful trip. In order to prevent such events, our options are limited. In fact, medication is the only real solution when it comes to facing symptoms. With this in mind, we have created a way for every adventurer to equip themselves properly according to their destination. A personalized medicine kit built for YOUR needs will certainly help to make sure ur trip goes according to plan

Changes of plan are inevitable when it comes to traveling.
The question is: are you prepared to face it?

The essentials

Here is a list of basic needs that is included in every order (portions vary)

  • Acetaminophen
  • Ibuprofen
  • Antacid
  • Antihistamine
  • Imodium
  • Melatonin
  • Powder for rehydration solution
  • Hydrocortisone cream
  • Antibiotic ointment
